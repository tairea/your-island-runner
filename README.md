# Your Island Runner

**A Galaxy Maps Curriculum for Roblox Studio Game Development**

Learn Roblox Studio by building an obstacle runner game based on your local island or town using satellite imagery as reference.

---

## Overview

**Target Audience**: Intermediate and high school students, complete beginners
**Context**: Classroom setting, weekly lessons over a school term
**Total Duration**: 20-25 hours (main path) + 10 hours (optional side-quests)
**Final Outcome**: A published Roblox obstacle runner game based on your local area

### What Makes This Curriculum Special

- **Personalized Geography**: Each student creates an obstacle course based on the shape and landmarks of their local island, city, or town using satellite imagery (Google Maps) as reference
- **Project-Based Learning**: Building one cohesive game throughout the entire course
- **Real-World Connection**: Students' games are unique and meaningful, making sharing with friends more exciting
- **Complete Workflow**: From concept to published, playable Roblox game

---

## Curriculum Structure

### Star 1: Quick Start Runner
*Build and play a minimal obstacle course in your first session*

- Mission 1.1 - Instant Setup - Download and install Roblox Studio, open a baseplate, and learn the Play/Stop buttons
- Mission 1.2 - Three-Jump Course - Place 4 parts to create your first playable jumping sequence
- Mission 1.3 - Add Danger - Create one kill brick that sends you back to start when touched
- Mission 1.4 - Victory Part - Add a finish line part in a different color to mark completion
- Mission 1.5 - Play Your Game - Test your mini-course and share your screen with the class

### Star 2: Island Vision
*Research and plan your personalized island runner*

- Mission 2.1 - Satellite Exploration - Navigate Google Maps to find the best satellite view of your local area
- Mission 2.2 - Shape Trace - Identify the distinctive outline shape of your island or town boundaries
- Mission 2.3 - Landmark Hunt - Select 5 real locations that will become obstacle stations
- Mission 2.4 - Story Your Route - Create a narrative reason for running through these landmarks

### Star 3: World Building Round 1
*Expand your course into an island-shaped world*

- Mission 3.1 - Reference Image Setup - Import your satellite screenshot as a decal in the world to guide building
- Mission 3.2 - Terrain Foundations - Use parts or terrain to create the rough shape of your island from the reference
- Mission 3.3 - Part Properties Deep Dive - Explore materials, colors, and transparency for variety
- Mission 3.4 - First Landmark Zone - Transform your first real landmark into an obstacle area
- Mission 3.5 - Connect the Path - Build a running path from spawn to your first landmark

### Star 4: Scripting Essentials
*Add real game mechanics with Lua code*

- Mission 4.1 - Scripts and Output - Create a Script, write print(), and find it in the Output window
- Mission 4.2 - Touched Events - Write a script that responds when players touch a part
- Mission 4.3 - Checkpoint Spawns - Script spawn points that save player progress
- Mission 4.4 - Death and Respawn - Code kill bricks to return players to checkpoints
- Mission 4.5 - End Goal Detection - Script your finish zone to recognize when players complete the course

### Star 5: World Building Round 2
*Complete all landmark zones with advanced obstacles*

- Mission 5.1 - Landmark Zone 2 - Build your second landmark as a distinct obstacle challenge
- Mission 5.2 - Landmark Zone 3 - Create your third landmark obstacle area
- Mission 5.3 - Moving Platforms - Add TweenService-powered moving parts to one zone
- Mission 5.4 - Landmark Zones 4-5 - Complete remaining landmark areas with varied challenges
- Mission 5.5 - Flow Testing - Play through the entire course and adjust difficulty curves

### Star 6: Polish Round
*Enhance visuals and fix rough edges*

- Mission 6.1 - Lighting and Atmosphere - Configure Lighting settings for mood (time of day, fog, etc.)
- Mission 6.2 - Skybox Selection - Choose and apply a skybox that fits your island theme
- Mission 6.3 - Detail Parts - Add small decorative elements that reference your real location
- Mission 6.4 - Sound Atmosphere - Add ambient audio to enhance immersion
- Mission 6.5 - Difficulty Tuning - Adjust obstacle spacing and timing based on playtest feedback

### Star 7: Launch Day
*Publish, share, and celebrate your creation*

- Mission 7.1 - Final Bug Hunt - Systematic playtest to find and fix remaining issues
- Mission 7.2 - Publishing Setup - Configure game settings, thumbnail, and description
- Mission 7.3 - Go Live - Publish to Roblox and verify the shareable link works
- Mission 7.4 - Class Showcase - Present your island story and watch classmates play
