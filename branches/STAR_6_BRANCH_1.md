# Branch: Particle Effects Magic

**Branch Objective**: Create eye-catching particle effects that bring your landmarks to life with fire, water spray, sparkles, and fog

**Why This Branch?**: Particle effects transform static environments into dynamic, living worlds. They're the secret ingredient in professional-looking Roblox games.

---

- Mission B6.1.1 - ParticleEmitter Basics - Understand core properties: Rate, Lifetime, Speed, Size, and Transparency curves
- Mission B6.1.2 - Fire and Torches - Create convincing flame effects with color gradients and upward velocity
- Mission B6.1.3 - Water and Mist - Add waterfall spray, beach wave mist, or morning fog to your island landmarks
- Mission B6.1.4 - Victory Celebration - Design an explosion of particles that triggers when the player completes your game
