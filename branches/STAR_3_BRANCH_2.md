# Branch: Part Organization Systems

**Branch Objective**: Implement a folder and naming system that keeps your game organized as it grows in complexity

**Why This Branch?**: As your world grows, finding specific parts becomes difficult. Professional builders use organization systems that save hours of frustration later.

---

- Mission B3.2.1 - Folder Hierarchy - Create a logical folder structure (Terrain, Landmarks, Obstacles, Checkpoints) in Workspace
- Mission B3.2.2 - Naming Conventions - Establish consistent naming patterns (Landmark_Beach_PalmTree_01) for easy searching
- Mission B3.2.3 - Model Grouping - Group related parts into Models for easy selection, movement, and duplication
