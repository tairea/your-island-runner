# Branch: Player Feedback Loop

**Branch Objective**: Establish a system for collecting player feedback, analyzing it, and making targeted improvements to your game

**Why This Branch?**: Professional game developers continuously improve their games based on player feedback. Learning this loop early builds a valuable mindset for any creative project.

---

- Mission B7.2.1 - Watch Players Play - Observe classmates playing your game without helping them, noting where they get confused or frustrated
- Mission B7.2.2 - Collect Feedback - Create simple questions to ask players about their experience (favorite part, hardest part, suggestions)
- Mission B7.2.3 - Prioritize Changes - Sort feedback into quick fixes, medium projects, and future ideas based on impact and effort
