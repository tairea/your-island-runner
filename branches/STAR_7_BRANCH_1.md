# Branch: Game Icon and Thumbnail

**Branch Objective**: Design and create a professional game icon and thumbnail that makes players want to click on your game

**Why This Branch?**: Your icon and thumbnail are the first things players see. Great marketing images can be the difference between 10 plays and 10,000 plays.

---

- Mission B7.1.1 - Camera Composition - Find the most impressive angle of your island using Studio's camera controls
- Mission B7.1.2 - Screenshot Techniques - Take high-quality screenshots using Roblox's built-in screenshot tool and hiding the UI
- Mission B7.1.3 - Icon Best Practices - Apply design principles (bright colors, clear focus, readable at small sizes) to your 512x512 icon
- Mission B7.1.4 - Thumbnail Storytelling - Create a thumbnail that shows action and excitement while representing your game accurately
