# Mission 1.5: Play Your Game

**Learning Objective**: Test your complete mini-course, make adjustments based on playtesting, and share your creation with classmates

## Summary
You did it - you built a game! Now it is time to play it for real, experience it as a player, and share your creation with your classmates. In this mission, you will do a complete playthrough of your obstacle course, note what works and what needs fixing, and then show off your work. This is the reward for all your building.

## Key Concepts
- Playtesting reveals problems you cannot see while building
- Iteration means making improvements based on testing
- Sharing your work is part of being a game developer
- Feedback from others helps you improve
- Every game developer started with something simple

## Prerequisites
- Completed Missions 1.1-1.4 (have a complete mini obstacle course)
- Course has: platforms, at least one kill brick, and a finish line
