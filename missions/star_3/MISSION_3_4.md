# Mission 3.4: First Landmark Zone

**Learning Objective**: Transform one landmark from your satellite reference into an interactive obstacle area with gameplay challenges

## Summary
Select one landmark from your island reference and build it as a playable obstacle zone. Learn the fundamentals of gameplay-focused building by creating structures that are both recognizable as your real-world landmark and fun to navigate as platforming challenges.

## Key Concepts
- Translating real landmarks into gameplay spaces
- Obstacle design principles (jump height, spacing, difficulty)
- Balancing visual authenticity with playability
- Part arrangement for player movement
- Testing and iteration in game design

## Prerequisites
- Completed Mission 3.3 (Part Properties Deep Dive)
- Understanding of part manipulation and properties
- Identified landmarks from Star 2 satellite research
- Basic understanding of platforming games
