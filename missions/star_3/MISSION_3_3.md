# Mission 3.3: Part Properties Deep Dive

**Learning Objective**: Apply materials, colors, and transparency to parts to create visual variety and environmental realism in your island world

## Summary
Transform your basic gray terrain parts into believable island environments by exploring Roblox's material, color, and transparency systems. Learn how these properties work together to create visual interest and communicate what different areas of your world represent.

## Key Concepts
- Part materials and their visual characteristics
- Color theory for environment design
- Transparency and visual layering
- Reflectance and surface properties
- Using properties to convey meaning and atmosphere

## Prerequisites
- Completed Mission 3.2 (Terrain Foundations)
- Understanding of the Properties panel
- Basic terrain parts created and organized
