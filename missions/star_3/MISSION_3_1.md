# Mission 3.1: Reference Image Setup

**Learning Objective**: Import a satellite screenshot as a decal in Roblox Studio to use as a building guide for your island world

## Summary
Transform your satellite screenshot from Star 2 into a physical reference decal placed flat on the ground in your Roblox world. This in-world reference will guide your building process as you recreate your island's shape and landmarks.

## Key Concepts
- Importing images into Roblox Studio
- Creating and configuring decals
- Using reference images for 3D building
- Workspace organization for worldbuilding

## Prerequisites
- Completed Star 2 (Island Vision) with saved satellite screenshot
- Basic familiarity with Roblox Studio Explorer and Properties panels
- Understanding of the 3D workspace from Star 1
