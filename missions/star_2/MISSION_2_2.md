# Mission 2.2: Shape Trace

**Learning Objective**: Identify and trace the distinctive outline shape of your local area's natural or human-made boundaries

## Summary
Analyze your satellite image to identify the natural or human-made boundaries that define your local area's shape. Learn to recognize geographic features like coastlines, rivers, major roads, and park boundaries that create distinctive outlines suitable for game world borders.

## Key Concepts
- Geographic boundaries (natural vs. human-made)
- Shape recognition and spatial analysis
- Game world boundary design
- Scale and proportion in map reading
- Negative space and land definition

## Prerequisites
- Completed Mission 2.1: Satellite Exploration
- Satellite image screenshot saved
- Basic understanding of maps and boundaries
