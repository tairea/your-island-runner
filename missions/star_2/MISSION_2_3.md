# Mission 2.3: Landmark Hunt

**Learning Objective**: Select 5 distinctive real-world locations within your island boundaries that will become obstacle stations in your game

## Summary
Identify and select 5 recognizable locations within your island boundaries that will serve as obstacle stations in your island runner game. Learn to evaluate locations based on visual distinctiveness, spatial distribution, and gameplay potential while considering how these landmarks connect to create an engaging route.

## Key Concepts
- Landmark identification and selection
- Point of interest (POI) placement in game design
- Spatial distribution and balance
- Visual distinctiveness in satellite imagery
- Route flow and pacing
- Real-world to game-world translation

## Prerequisites
- Completed Mission 2.1: Satellite Exploration
- Completed Mission 2.2: Shape Trace
- Satellite image with traced boundaries
- Understanding of your island's shape and scale
