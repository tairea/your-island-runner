# Mission 2.1: Satellite Exploration

**Learning Objective**: Navigate Google Maps satellite view to locate and capture a clear aerial image of your local area

## Summary
Learn to navigate Google Maps in satellite view to find the best aerial perspective of your neighborhood, town, or island. Master the controls to zoom, pan, and tilt the view, then capture a screenshot that will serve as the foundation for your island runner game design.

## Key Concepts
- Satellite imagery and aerial photography
- Map navigation controls (zoom, pan, tilt)
- Geographic orientation and scale
- Screenshot capture techniques
- Image quality and resolution considerations

## Prerequisites
- Internet connection
- Access to Google Maps
- Basic computer navigation skills
- Ability to take screenshots on your device
