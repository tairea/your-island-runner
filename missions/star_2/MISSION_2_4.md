# Mission 2.4: Story Your Route

**Learning Objective**: Create a narrative context and logical route progression that connects your 5 landmarks into a cohesive running journey

## Summary
Develop a narrative framework that gives players a meaningful reason to run through your selected landmarks in a specific order. Learn to sequence locations for optimal gameplay flow, create story-driven motivation for the journey, and design a route that makes geographic and narrative sense.

## Key Concepts
- Narrative design and player motivation
- Route sequencing and flow
- Start and end point selection
- Story beats and pacing
- Geographic logic in routing
- Theme development
- Player journey arc

## Prerequisites
- Completed Mission 2.1: Satellite Exploration
- Completed Mission 2.2: Shape Trace
- Completed Mission 2.3: Landmark Hunt
- 5 selected landmarks marked on satellite image
- Landmark Registry with location details
