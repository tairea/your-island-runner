**Learning Objective**: Design and build a second landmark zone with distinct obstacle challenges that contrast with the first zone

## Summary
Now that you've mastered basic scripting, it's time to expand your island runner with a second landmark zone. You'll design a new obstacle challenge area that feels different from your first zone, creating variety in your game experience.

- Playtesting fundamentals
