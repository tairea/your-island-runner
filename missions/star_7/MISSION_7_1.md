# Mission 7.1: Final Bug Hunt

**Learning Objective**: Conduct systematic playtesting to identify and fix remaining issues before publication

## Summary
Before launching your game to the world, it's time for one final, thorough playtest. You'll use a systematic approach to hunt down any remaining bugs, test edge cases, and ensure your island runner delivers a smooth experience for every player who visits.

## Key Concepts
- Systematic testing methodology
- Edge case identification
- Quality assurance principles
- Bug prioritization
- User experience validation
- Pre-launch checklist

## Prerequisites
- Completed Star 6 (Polish Round)
- All major features implemented
- Game is playable from start to finish
- Ready for final testing phase
