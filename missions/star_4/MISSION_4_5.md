# Mission 4.5: End Goal Detection

**Learning Objective**: Script your finish zone to recognize when players complete the course

## Summary
Create the ultimate reward - a finish line that celebrates when players complete your island runner! Learn how to detect game completion, provide satisfying feedback, track wins on a leaderboard, and give players a sense of accomplishment. This mission completes your game's core loop.

## Key Concepts
- Win conditions define how players complete your game
- Celebration feedback makes victory feel rewarding
- Tables can track which players have already won
- GUI elements can display victory messages
- Sound and visual effects enhance player experience

## Prerequisites
- Completion of Mission 4.4 (Death and Respawn)
- Working checkpoint and kill brick systems
- Understanding of Touched events and player detection
